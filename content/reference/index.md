## TL;DR

Reference pages are not teachers.

They are tools for readers who already know enough context to ask a precise question.

## What a good reference page should contain

- one-sentence definition
- strong use and avoid guidance
- comparisons
- common gotchas
- links to the right tutorial or how-to when the reader needs more than reference
