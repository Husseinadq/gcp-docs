## TL;DR

If you skip the core concepts, GCP feels more confusing than it really is.

The most important concepts to learn first are:

- IAM
- projects
- billing
- regions
- service accounts

## Why this section matters

Product docs often assume you already understand these concepts. In reality, these are the concepts that explain why something works, why it fails, and who is allowed to change it.

## Start here

Read [IAM basics](/docs/core-concepts/iam-basics) before making production access decisions. It is the concept most likely to block teams early.
