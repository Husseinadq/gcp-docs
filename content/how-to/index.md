## TL;DR

Use this section when you can finish the sentence:

"I need to do _this exact thing_ on GCP."

That is what a how-to guide is for.

## What a good how-to guide must contain

- assumptions
- prerequisites
- exact sequence
- stop condition
- the most common failure points

If the page is trying to teach the whole platform, it is no longer a good how-to guide.
